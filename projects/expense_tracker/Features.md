## Key Features

### 1. Add Expenses
- Track amount, category, description, and date
- Validate categories with warnings for custom ones

---

### 2. List & Filter Expenses
- View all expenses or filter by category
- Limit results and filter by date range (last N days)
- Sort expenses by date (newest first)

---

### 3. Delete Expenses
- Remove expenses by ID with confirmation

---

### 4. Generate Reports
- Summary statistics:
  - Total spending
  - Average spending
  - Transaction count
- Spending breakdown by category with percentages
- Supported time periods:
  - Week
  - Month
  - Year
  - All time

---

### 5. Visualize Spending
- ASCII bar charts showing spending by category
- Visual comparison of category spending

---

### 6. Standard Categories
- Food  
- Transportation  
- Entertainment  
- Shopping  
- Bills  
- Healthcare  
- Education  
- Other
